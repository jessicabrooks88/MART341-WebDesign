Night of Girl is an immersive gaming experience designed by Jessica Brooks. It is a third-person PC game that follows the protagonist Andie through point- and-click puzzles that will guarantee her escape if she succeeds and defeats her captors' cronies. She starts the game trapped in a billionaire's basement and spends the duration going through each level, equipping unlikely objects as weapons, trying to get outside to the woods. Here, she must ultimately kill her kidnapper, the elusive Mr. Hands. The game has a sense of humor with a pop culture flair that sets it apart from other horror games and grabs influence from past point-and-click games like Myst and Harvester with a cute aesthetic from RPG's like Story of Seasons to create a creepy contrast. It is rated M for Violence and Strong Language.
